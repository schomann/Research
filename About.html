About

Timo Schomann, PhD

Timo is an experience postdoctoral researcher in the field of Biomedical Science, which involves working on various projects in a multidisciplinary team. As a result, Timo is skilled in stem cell culture and differentiation into, e.g., neural cells, bone, and cartilage. In addition, Timo is experienced in cancer therapy, drug development in form of bio-compatible nanomaterials and molecular imaging.
Currently, Timo is holding both a Senior Researcher position at Percuros B.V. as well as a Postdoctoral Researcher position at the Surgery Department of Leiden University Medical Center (LUMC), which gives him the opportunity to work on projects involving, e.g., stem cells, bacterial nanocellulose, CO-releasing molecules, and extracellular vesicles, to treat diseases, such as hearing loss, (gastrointestinal) cancer, or hypertension.
The main focus of Timo's research is the translation of therapies into the clinic as a marketable product.
